About Me
I joined Peking Univeristy in Fall 2019 as an assistant professor of EECS. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Imperial College London under the supervision of Yike Guo in Fall 2019. My research involves deep learning and computer vision with the goal of reducing the data required for learning intelligent systems. I am passionate about popularizing artificial intelligence technologies and established TensorLayer, a deep learning and reinforcement learning library for scientists and engineers, which won the Best Open Source Software Award at ACM Multimedia 2017. Before Ph.D., I received my MSc degree with distinction from Imperial in 2012, and a first-class BEng degree from the University of Central Lancashire in 2011. I founded a startup for digital healthcare between 2012 and 2014 with Yike Guo.

Open positions available (Ph.D., PostDocs, Interns). Contact me for details.
